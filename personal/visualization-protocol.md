# Visualization Protocol (6 Components)

## 1. Center Yourself
- Get to emptiness before creating something
- Breathing → Witnessing → Realization
- Detach from predetermined script

## 2. Visualize the Goal
- Begin with the end in mind
- Best time: right before bed
- 15-30 second burst of visualization
- Focus on what money can BUY, not the amount
- Example: Hit million subscriber → hundreds of thousands → people working with us

## 3. Visualize The Process
- Don't worry about HOW
- 20% time: Goal visualization (gives insights)
- 80% time: Process visualization (causes movement)
- Throughout the day, before performing a task

## 4. Visualize the Ideal Self
- "If I were to achieve this goal effortlessly, who would I be?"
- BE. DO. HAVE.
- Step outside your body, visualize ideal self, step in

## 5. Aligned Action
- Visualization is internal action
- Combined with external action = manifestation
- BEING → DOING → HAVING

## When to Visualize
- Before bed: GOAL
- Throughout the day: PROCESS
- Morning: VISUALIZE
