# Buffett's 5 Investing Rules (Balance Sheet Analysis)

## Rule 1: Cash vs. Debt
- Formula: Cash > Total Debt
- Goal: More cash than debt

## Rule 2: Debt-to-Equity
- Formula: Total Liabilities / Shareholders' Equity
- Goal: Ratio below 0.8

## Rule 3: Preferred Stock
- Goal: No preferred stock on the balance sheet

## Rule 4: Retained Earnings Growth
- Goal: Consistently growing retained earnings, even during recessions

## Rule 5: Treasury Stock
- Goal: Positive treasury stock (stock buybacks)

## Example: Chipotle
- Rule 1: Pass ($1.42B cash, zero debt)
- Rule 2: Fail (1.2 ratio) — nuanced fail
- Rule 3: Pass (no preferred stock)
- Rule 4: Pass
- Rule 5: Pass ($5B treasury stock)
