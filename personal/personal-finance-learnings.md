# Personal Finance Learnings

## Common Myths
1. Real Estate only goes up
2. Stocks are riskier than Real Estate
3. LIC gives better returns after maturity
4. Risk appetite depends on age
5. Investing means stock picking
6. Buy investment products from well-known companies

## Asset Allocation by Time Horizon
| Horizon | Asset Class |
|---------|------------|
| Short term | Debt (liquid) |
| Medium term | Fixed Deposits |
| Long term | Equity (index fund) |

## Mutual Fund Selection (Index over Active)
- Invest in **Nifty 50 Index Fund** not active Large Cap MF
- Use Tickertape screener: AUM min 5000 Cr, sort by expense ratio
- Track rolling returns at advisorkhoj.com
- Gap between index and MF = Alpha (less alpha = better)

## Debt Investing Optimization
- Senior Citizen: First 50K FD interest tax-free
- Equity Saver Fund: ~10% return, taxation like equity
- Tax is only 12.5% on some debt instruments

## Gold (Never Buy Jewelry)
- **Sovereign Gold Bonds** (RBI-backed, 8-year holding)
- Buy old SGB on Zerodha for better pricing
- Physical gold = 15-20% loss on making charges + 3% GST

## US Market
- Diversification + USD appreciation
- Invest via index funds / ETF

## Insurance Traps
- Don't mix insurance with investment
- Buy term insurance separately
- Health insurance is separate from life insurance
