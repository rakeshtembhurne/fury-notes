## Final Teachings

The most powerful affirmation practice is not pretending you have something you don't. It is recognizing the truth of what you already are at the deepest level - and then letting that recognition pull your actions forward into alignment with it.

The Buddha did not become enlightened. He recognized what was always already true. Your affirmations work the same way. You are not lying to yourself. You are remembering what you actually are - and training your daily mind to act from that remembering.

That is the practice. That is the path.
