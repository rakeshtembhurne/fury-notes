## How AI Search Actually Works

| Old Search | AI Search |
|-----------|-----------|
| AI scans webpages | AI queries Google's entity database (54B entities, 1.6T facts) |
| Matches keywords | Matches entity definitions |
| Ranks pages | Cites authoritative entities |

If you're in the database as a clearly defined entity → you get cited.
If you're not clearly defined → you don't exist to AI.

## Three Schema Types Every Business Needs Minimum

| Schema Type | What It Defines | Example |
|------------|----------------|---------|
| **Organization Schema** | Your company entity | Company name, logo, founders, social profiles |
| **Product/Service Schema** | What you offer | Product type, attributes, use cases |
| **Local Business Schema** | Where you operate | Address, service area, hours, service types |
