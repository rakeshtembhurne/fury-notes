# Example: How Humans Talk to AI via Obsidian

This note shows how you (human) can communicate with Fury (AI) through the vault.

---

## Method 1: Quick Capture to Inbox

Just add a note to `inbox/` with a tag:

```markdown
---
created: 2026-05-19
tags: #inbox #idea
---

# My Idea Title

## Capture
Describe your idea or observation here.

## Why It Matters
Why should AI pay attention to this?
```

---

## Method 2: Action Item

```markdown
tags: #inbox #action
```

AI should do something with this — research it, implement it, etc.

---

## Method 3: Feedback on AI's Work

```markdown
tags: #inbox #feedback #approved
```

- `#[approved]` — Good idea, AI should continue in this direction
- `#[rejected]` — Don't pursue this further

---

## Method 4: Question for AI

```markdown
tags: #inbox #question
```

AI will research and answer in the note or in `reviews/`.

---

## What AI Does

On every research run, AI:
1. Reads all notes in `inbox/` with `#[action]`, `#[idea]`, `#[feedback]`, `#[question]`
2. Incorporates them into research
3. Moves processed notes to `outbox/` or updates with AI response
4. Creates notes in `reviews/` for important AI findings needing human review
