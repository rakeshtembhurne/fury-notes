# Distribution Strategy — Brandsome Projects
> Source: Tweet learnings digest + Rocky decisions
> Date: 2026-05-23

---

## Core Thesis
> "Building an app ≠ making money. Getting to $10K/mo takes a year+ of marketing, testing, experiments. Building is the easy part."
> "Distribution is the bottleneck."

**We solve distribution FIRST, not after building.**

---

## Action Items (Confirmed by Rocky)

### 1. Web Funnels — BYPASS Apple 30% Tax
- **Reference:** "Cal AI founder — $2M/mo. Using web funnels instead of App Store = $1.9M vs $1.4M after Apple's 30% cut."
- **Decision:** All our SaaS products → web funnels first
- App Store ONLY after validation and revenue
- Use tools like FunnelFox or equivalent to integrate

### 2. Lifetime Deals on Website
- **Reference:** "Lifetime deals work for iOS — monthly $4.99, yearly $19.99, lifetime $24.99. More users buy lifetime."
- **Decision:** Implement lifetime pricing on brandsome.dev and brandsome.app
- Landing page → pricing → lifetime deal upsell

### 3. Build in Public — Aggressive Social Media
- **Reference:** "Build in public = future search engine for your own journey"
- **Decision:** Active on Twitter/X, LinkedIn, maybe Reddit
- Document launches, revenue milestones, failures
- Organic growth through transparency

### 4. Reference Product — PDF to Excel Tool ($40K/mo)
- **Reference:** "$40K/mo. ONE simple tool — turns PDF bank statements into Excel. 39/40 is profit. Playbook: solve YOUR own problem first."
- **Decision:** Keep this playbook in mind for brandsome.dev
- Simple tools, solve ONE problem deeply, high margin
