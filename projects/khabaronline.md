# KhabarOnline.com

## Project Overview

Automated news aggregation and content rewriting platform. Domain registered: khabaronline.in (not .com)

## The Concept

### Problem
Creating fresh news content consistently is time-consuming and resource-intensive.

### Solution
1. Monitor news sources (Times of India, other news sites)
2. Scrape/tracking trending news articles
3. AI rewrites articles in original voice/style
4. Auto-publish to blog platform

### How It Worked (Previous Version)
- Built on Blogger platform
- UI that connected to Blogger APIs
- Scraped news from Times of India and other sites
- Rewrote articles using AI
- Auto-published to website

### Use Cases
- News aggregator websites
- Regional news portals
- Niche news sites
- Content marketing

## Previous Stack (Blogger Version)

- Blogger platform (blogspot)
- Blogger REST APIs
- News scraping (Times of India, etc.)
- AI rewriting

## Possible New Stack

- Next.js frontend
- AI for content rewriting
- CMS or headless CMS
- SEO optimized
- monetization ready
