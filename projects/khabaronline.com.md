# KhabarOnline.com Playbook

> Related: [[khabar-online-content-gap]] · [[content-strategy]] · [[ai-reddit-content-research-method]]

Automated news aggregation and content rewriting platform.

## Project Status

**Status:** 🟡 Planning
**Domain:** khabaronline.in (registered, not .com)

Previous proof-of-concept exists on Blogger. Project is in planning to rebuild with modern stack.

## Concept

### Problem
Creating fresh news content consistently is time-consuming and resource-intensive.

### Solution
1. Monitor news sources (Times of India, other news sites)
2. Scrape/tracking trending news articles
3. AI rewrites articles in original voice/style
4. Auto-publish to blog platform

### Use Cases
- News aggregator websites
- Regional news portals
- Niche news sites
- Content marketing

## Previous Stack (Blogger Version)

- Blogger platform (blogspot)
- Blogger REST APIs
- News scraping (Times of India, etc.)
- AI rewriting
- Auto-published to website
