## Channel Strategy

1. **Instagram** — Primary. Visual-first. Reels for reach, carousel for depth, stories for intimacy.
2. **Pinterest** — Long-tail discovery. Evergreen pins outperform IG over 6 months.
3. **WhatsApp** — Retention engine. Repeat buyer concierge. High-intent channel.
4. **YouTube Shorts** — Experimental. Test styling explainers.
5. **Blog** — SEO play. "Best linen shirt India", "how to style kurta for office" long-tail.

## Objection Handlers

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Can't feel the fabric online" | Free first-return. Keep what fits, return what doesn't. We pay shipping both ways on first order. |
| "Sizing is inconsistent" | Published size chart + exact measurements per garment. If size runs small, we say so. |
| "What if it fades?" | 30-day fabric guarantee. Show us the fade — full refund. |
| "Too similar to Zara" | Zara is volume + turnover. We're curated + repeatable. Buy a Trigyaa shirt in April, restock the exact same cut in October. |
| "Shipping takes forever" | 3-5 days metro, 5-7 days tier-2. Tracked. We message you personally on dispatch. |

## Competitors & Wedge

- **Competitors:** Zara/H&M (volume), Westside (department-store convenience), Local boutiques (exclusivity markup)
- **Wedge:** Repeatability + fabric quality at ₹799-2000 price band. Nobody else delivers both.

## Glossary

- **"Drop":** Limited curated release (5-10 new pieces), not a collection or season
- **"Repeatable":** A cut that will be restocked, not discontinued. North star.
- **"Honest sizing":** Published bust/waist/hip in cm, not S/M/L guesswork
- **"Fabric-first":** Every design decision starts with fabric, not silhouette
