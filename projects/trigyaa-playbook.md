# Trigyaa Playbook

> Related: [[trigyaa-content-calendar-30day]] · [[trigyaa-hermes-content-prompts]] · [[trigyaa-flux-ai-image-workflow]] · [[flux-ai-social-media-automation]]

Minimalist women's wear. Urban India. D2C. Premium feel, honest price.

## Story

Trigyaa started because Sneha kept returning clothes — fabric disappointing after one wash, fit inconsistent across same brand's sizes, and every boutique pricing felt like they charge you for the white walls not the dress. Built shop.trigyaa.com to sell what we'd actually wear: simple, well-made, fair-priced.

## Voice & Tone

**Would write:**
1. "New drop: three linen shirts. One for Monday. One for Friday night. One that handles both."
2. "The pockets are real. The hemline lasts. The price is ₹1299."
3. "Why do most tops shrink? Because the yarn is pre-shrunk for a 65°C wash. Ours is for 40°C. That's why."

**Would NOT write:**
1. "BUY NOW! 80% OFF! LIMITED TIME!"
2. "Slay queen 💅 this look is EVERYTHING 🔥"
3. Long paragraphs of vague adjectives ("stunning, elegant, breathtaking")

**Words we use:** fabric, fit, cut, everyday, premium, honest, simple, crisp, drape
**Words we avoid:** luxury, exclusive, ultimate, amazing, perfect, revolutionary
**Formality (0-10):** 4
**Humor (0-10):** 3 (dry, observational, never forced)
**Emoji policy:** Max 1 per post. Natural ones only (🧵, ✂️, 🌿). No 🔥💅✨.

## Content Pillars

### Pillar 1: Outfit of the Day
- Angle: Real women, real mornings, real outfits. No studio gloss.
- Example hook: "6:45 AM. Meeting at 9. This is the shirt I reach for."
- ICP pain addressed: "Looking put-together without thinking about it"

### Pillar 2: Behind the Seam
- Angle: Show why a garment lasts. Fabric, stitch, cut.
- Example hook: "Zoom in. This is a 60s double-needle hem. That's why it doesn't unravel at month 6."
- ICP pain: "Fast fashion falling apart"

### Pillar 3: Customer Love
- Angle: Real reviews, real UGC. Not influencer gifting.
- Example hook: "Priya's been wearing our linen shirt every Monday for 3 months. Here's the wash count."
- ICP pain: "Will it actually last?"

### Pillar 4: Styling Tips
- Angle: One piece, many occasions. Reduce purchase guilt.
- Example hook: "This kurta: brunch, office, date night. Same piece. Three hours of styling."
- ICP pain: "Wardrobe fatigue"

## Launch Plan

Trigyaa is already live. These launch actions apply to **new drops**, not initial launch.

### Pre-drop (T-7 days)
- [ ] Teaser reel (no product reveal, just fabric texture close-up)
- [ ] Email list: "Drop preview — 48hr early access for subscribers"
- [ ] WhatsApp broadcast to repeat buyers
- [ ] 3 styling shots ready

### Drop day
- [ ] Live product pages (T-0)
- [ ] IG reel reveal (9 AM IST)
- [ ] Story countdown + swipe-up
- [ ] Pinterest pins go live
- [ ] WhatsApp link to repeat buyers
- [ ] Email blast to full list (6 PM IST)

### Week 1 post-drop
- [ ] Reply to every comment, every DM
- [ ] Daily story showing customer photos (with permission)
- [ ] UGC collection → feature week 2
- [ ] Restock alert for fast-selling sizes

## Channel Strategy

- **Instagram:** Primary. Visual-first, Indian women 25-40 spend time here. Reels for reach, carousel for depth, stories for intimacy.
- **Pinterest:** Long-tail discovery. Fashion Pinterest is underutilized in India — evergreen pins outperform IG posts over 6 months.
- **WhatsApp:** Retention engine. Repeat buyer concierge. High-intent channel.
- **YouTube Shorts:** Experimental. Test styling explainers.
- **Blog (marketing.trigyaa.com):** SEO play. "Best linen shirt India", "how to style kurta for office" long-tail.
- **Twitter/LinkedIn:** Not primary ICP hang-out. Skip for now.

### Objection handlers

| Objection | Response |
|---|---|
| "Can't feel the fabric online" | Free first-return. Keep what fits, return what doesn't. We pay shipping both ways on first order. |
| "Sizing is inconsistent in Indian brands" | Published size chart + exact measurements per garment. If a size runs small, we say so. |
| "What if it fades?" | 30-day fabric guarantee. Show us the fade — full refund. |
| "Too similar to Zara" | Zara is volume + turnover. We're curated + repeatable. Buy a Trigyaa shirt in April, restock the exact same cut in October. |
| "Shipping takes forever" | 3-5 days metro, 5-7 days tier-2. Tracked. We message you personally on dispatch. |

## Competitors & Wedge

- **They:** Zara/H&M sell volume at scale. Westside sells department-store convenience. Local boutiques sell exclusivity markup.
- **We:** Sell repeatable essentials that last. Same cut available 6 months later. Owned supply chain = honest pricing.
- **Wedge:** Repeatability + fabric quality at ₹799-2000 price band. Nobody else in this band delivers both.

## Glossary / Lore

- **"Drop":** Limited curated release. Not a collection. Not a season. Just 5-10 new pieces.
- **"Repeatable":** A cut that will be restocked, not discontinued. Our north star.
- **"Honest sizing":** Published bust/waist/hip in cm, not S/M/L guesswork.
- **"Fabric-first":** Every design decision starts with fabric, not silhouette.
