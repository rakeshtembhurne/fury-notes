## Key Findings

### The Core Pattern in Indie Hacking

1. **"Boring" vertical apps >> "sexy" horizontal tools** — Parking finder $60K MRR, toll calculator $45K MRR, fuel tracker $50K MRR
2. **Micro SaaS exits at 17-33x MRR** — Slack Bot sold at 33x, Reddit Scraper at 28x
3. **AI is a tool, not the product** — No one is selling "AI business agents" successfully
4. **Validate before building** — Tweet first, code second, get paying customer in 2 weeks
5. **Complexity is the moat** — Simple questions = AI answers directly. Complex questions = AI needs expert sources.
