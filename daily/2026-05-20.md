## trigyaa-content-calendar-30day.md

**Purpose:** Monthly IG content calendar for Trigyaa with specific post types, hooks, timing per day.

**Structure:**
- 4-week cycle with 7 posts/week
- Mix: 3x OOTD, 2x BTS, 1x Customer Love, 1x Styling Tips + variety
- Drop week modification (T-7 to T+7) — replaces mix with product-focused content
- Posting times: Reels 9AM IST, Carousels 12PM IST, Stories 6PM IST
- WhatsApp + Pinterest cadence included

**Key insight:** Drop weeks require a completely different content matrix — pre-launch teasers → launch → post-launch UGC
