## What Was Researched

### 1. Logo Tool Competitive Landscape (Brandsome.dev)

Researched 5 logo creation tools to understand market:
- **Brandmark.io** — One-time payment model, most comprehensive (logo + website + social + cards + animated). Key insight: one payment > subscription for branding.
- **LogoMakr** — Freemium, AI logo gen + canvas editor
- **Shopify Hatchful** — Free, 13K templates, loss leader for Shopify ecosystem
- **Looka** — Blocked by bot detection
- **Logo.com** — Blocked by bot detection

**Key finding:** No competitor has brand → content pipeline. Logo tools do logos, Canva does design. The gap: logo creation → brand kit → AI content gen.

**Tech stack insight:** AI logo gen = templates + Flux AI for icons + Google Fonts + color theory engine.

### 2. Social Media SaaS Competitive Landscape (Brandsome.app)

Researched Canva's pricing and brand management features (canva.com/business/features/brand):
- Canva Pro: ₹4,000/yr (~$48), 5 Brand Kits, social scheduling
- Canva Business: ₹6,800/yr (~$82), 100 Brand Kits, brand controls, approvals
- Canva's Brand Kit: logos, fonts, colors in toolbar, brand templates, brand controls
- Canva's Magic Switch: auto-resize for all platforms
- Canva's weakness: still manual design, AI is add-on not core workflow

**Key finding:** Canva has the brand features but not the AI content automation. "Paste brand → Get content" workflow doesn't exist.

## Key Insights

### Brandsome.dev (Logo Tool)
1. **Brandmark.io is the model** — one-time payment, lifetime access, comprehensive brand suite
2. **AI logo gen = templates + Flux AI** — not as complex as it sounds
3. **Differentiation = brand kit extraction** — logo → brand kit → feeds Brandsome.app
4. **Recommended pricing:** $29 one-time (Pro) + $9/mo optional for AI features

### Brandsome.app (Social Media SaaS)
1. **Canva Business is the threat** — ₹6,800/yr, 100 Brand Kits, but still manual design
2. **The gap:** "Paste brand → AI generates content" — no one does this
3. **Integrate don't compete:** Postiz for scheduling, Runway for video
4. **Total extra cost: ~$12/mo** (Runway Standard)

### Dogfood First
Both tools should be tested with Trigyaa BEFORE building as products. The research program already says this — today confirmed it's the right approach.

## Decisions Made

1. **Brandsome.dev first, then Brandsome.app** — brand kit is prerequisite for content gen
2. **Dogfood with Trigyaa before selling** — validate with real usage, not just research
3. **One-time payment model for Brandsome.dev** — Brandmark proved this works
4. **SaaS subscription for Brandsome.app** — ongoing value, AI features
