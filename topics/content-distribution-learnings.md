---

## Social Media Content Strategy

### Core Principles

1. **One source clip → 3 platforms** — TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts. Multiply distribution with zero marginal effort.
2. **Consistency beats virality** — Algorithm rewards consistent posting. Volume (3-5 clips/day) compounds over months.
3. **Pick one format, one niche, commit for 12 months** — YouTube growth rule. Don't dilute attention.
4. **The 6-month quit point** — When peers in your circle quit (usually month 6), treat it as competitive moat. Double down.

### Content Types That Work

| Type | Platform | Why |
|------|----------|-----|
| UGC-style short video | TikTok, Reels | Replicable, anyone can copy |
| Faceless health/fruit content | TikTok, IG, YT Shorts | High CPM, evergreen, shareable |
| AI persona + low-ticket product | All | Removes creator bottleneck |
| Engagement bait (like & comment X) | All | Triggers algorithm distribution |
| Live P&L transparency | Twitter, YouTube | Trust-building, unique content |

### AI UGC Workflow (Proven)
- Script → AI voiceover → auto-subtitles → 8 videos in 35 minutes
- Client paid $400 for 8 videos, got 145K+ views in 48 hours
- Value gap: $50/video to produce vs traditional costs
- Package as fixed-price service: 8 videos, $397, 48hr delivery

### YouTube Ranking (TSC Formula)
- **Title:** Use brackets/parentheses for higher CTR
- **Keywords:** 1-3 target + 1-3 synonym + 1-3 category
- **Description:** 100+ words, first 2-3 sentences most important, keyword 3-4x
- **Watch time:** Plan scripts, mix camera angles, change visual styles
- **Questions:** Ask multiple choice (not "leave a comment")

### LinkedIn Strategy
- Post every day
- Tell lots of stories
- Don't chase viral
- Yes to selfies, no to stock images
- Comment frequently on others' posts

### Email Platforms (For Outreach/Newsletter)
- **EmailOctopus:** Free 2.5K subscribers, 10K emails/month
- **MailerLite:** Free 1K subscribers, 12K emails/month
- **ListMonk:** Open source, low RAM (already self-hosted on Rocky server)
- **MailChimp:** Industry standard

### Free Image Sources
- Unsplash
- Pexels
- Freepik
- Wikipedia Commons

## Distribution Channels by Project Type

### E-commerce / D2C (Trigyaa model)
- **Primary:** Instagram (visual-first, carousel for depth, stories for intimacy)
- **Secondary:** Pinterest (evergreen, underutilized in India)
- **Retention:** WhatsApp (broadcast to repeat buyers)
- **Experimental:** YouTube Shorts (styling explainers)
- **SEO:** Blog (long-tail: "best linen shirt India", "how to style kurta for office")

### News / Content (KhabarOnline model)
- Auto-blog: scrape → AI rewrite → publish
- SEO-optimized, monetized
- Use ListMonk for email distribution

### Trading / Finance (TradeMyBots model)
- **Primary:** YouTube (long-form to evaluate bots)
- **Secondary:** Twitter/Fintwit India (P&L snapshots)
- **Community:** Reddit (r/IndiaInvestments, educational posts)
- **Alerts:** Telegram (daily updates for active traders)
- **SEO:** Blog ("algo trading India", "best trading bot India")
