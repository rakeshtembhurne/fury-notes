# 8 Months Running an Automation Agency — The Truth Nobody Tells You

## Key Insights

1. **Technical Skills Alone Don't Make an Agency** - While knowing how to build workflows is essential, success requires equal focus on communication, client acquisition, and relationship management. Many people underestimate the business development side.

2. **Client Issues Are Inevitable** - Expect scope creep, misaligned expectations, and ongoing support requests. Build buffer time and establish clear contracts with defined deliverables and revision limits to protect your time and profitability.

3. **Top Skills Needed Beyond Automation** - Effective client communication, project scoping, proposal writing, and the ability to translate technical capabilities into business value propositions for non-technical stakeholders.

4. **Pricing Strategy Matters** - Don't undervalue your work. Establish tiered pricing models (starter, growth, enterprise) and understand your cost of delivery. Charge for discovery calls and project planning upfront.

5. **Focus on Repeatable Systems** - Build template workflows you can adapt across clients rather than starting from scratch each time. Document your processes so you can delegate or scale without sacrificing quality.
