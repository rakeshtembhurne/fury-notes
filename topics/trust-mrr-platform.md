# TrustMRR — Micro-Startup Acquisition Platform

## What is TrustMRR

Marketplace for buying/selling micro-SaaS and small online businesses.
- Focus: MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) businesses
- Quick transactions (38 days average?)
- Trust-based escrow system

## How It Works

1. **List** your micro-startup with MRR
2. **Buyer** purchases through escrow
3. **Transfer** ownership
4. **TrustMRR** takes fee (~few %)

## Why TrustMRR Works

1. **Fast** — 38 days to sell
2. **Escrow** — Trust built in
3. **Focused** — MRR businesses only
4. **Community** — Indie hackers actively buying

## For AI Micro-Startups

- AI tools/apps could be sold here
- Even small MRR ($100-500) has value
- Speed of sale depends on:
  - Uniqueness
  - Growth trajectory
  - Technical stack (no-code = easier transfer)
