## Social Media Millionaire Playbook

### The 6 Non-Obvious Principles

1. **Narrow Focus**
   - Don't try to be everywhere
   - Pick ONE content type (short or long form video)
   - Pick ONE platform
   - Crack it for 6 months before expanding

2. **Systematize Before Scaling**
   - Figure out one content type first
   - Build system, then multiply

3. **Quality Over Quantity**
   - Better to post 3x week with system
   - Than 10x without

4. **Engage Obsessively**
   - Reply to every comment
   - Build community
   - DMs > broadcast

5. **Build In Public**
   - Share progress, revenue, learnings
   - Creates social proof
   - Attracts opportunity

6. **Monetize After Traction**
   - Don't sell too early
   - Build audience first
   - Multiple monetization paths later

## Key Principles

### Why Side Hustles Fail
1. Fear of what people think
2. Not connecting tools with ideas
3. Starting too big instead of too small
4. Not systematizing before scaling
5. Giving up too early

### What Winners Do Differently
1. Start despite fear
2. Connect existing tools to ideas
3. Start small, iterate fast
4. Build systems first
5. Persist through hard times
