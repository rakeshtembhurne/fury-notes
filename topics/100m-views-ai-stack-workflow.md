# The Complete AI Stack and Workflow for 100M+ Video Views

## Key Insights

1. **Short-Form Video Is Top of Funnel** - Short-form content (TikTok, Reels, Shorts) is the primary attention driver on the internet. Getting this right is essential for any business or creator looking to build an audience at scale.

2. **Workflow From Idea to Publishing** - The process is: idea → script → recording → editing → deployment. The fastest creators have streamlined this into muscle memory, moving from concept to published video in minimal time.

3. **$100K+ Studio Setup (But You Can Start Cheap)** - Pro creators invest heavily in gear and environment, but similar results are achievable with basic equipment. The key is the workflow and process, not expensive equipment.

4. **Tools Matter Less Than Process** - Understanding which tools to use is important, but mastering your personal workflow matters more. The same tools used differently produce dramatically different results.

5. **Behind-the-Scenes Transparency Sells** - Showing your process (how you research, script, film, edit) builds trust and differentiates your content. Audiences increasingly value authenticity over polish.
