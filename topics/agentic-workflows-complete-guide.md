# the n8n killer? AGENTIC WORKFLOWS: Full Beginner's Guide

## Key Insights

1. **Agentic Workflows Are the Future** - These are self-directed AI systems that maintain and improve themselves over time. They're not just automation—they're AI that decides what to do, uses tools, learns from mistakes, and improves without human intervention.

2. **Separation of Concerns with DOE Framework** - Directive (what goal?), Orchestration (which agent handles what?), Execution (actual task completion). This framework structures complex multi-agent systems for clarity and maintainability.

3. **Self-Healing Workflows** - Agentic workflows can detect when something goes wrong (API failure, unexpected output) and recover automatically—retrying, adjusting approach, or escalating to human review when necessary.

4. **Revenue Potential Is Massive** - The creator scaled two agencies to $160K+ combined revenue and runs a $300K/month profitable AI community. Agentic workflows are positioned to revolutionize business process automation.

5. **Practical, Actionable Focus** - The course prioritizes immediately applicable skills over theoretical concepts. Learn by building real systems that generate revenue rather than just understanding technology for technology's sake.
