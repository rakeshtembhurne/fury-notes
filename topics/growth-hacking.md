## The GEO Agency Opportunity

### The Market
- GEO market projected: **$7 billion**
- AI search traffic growing **500% YoY**
- Only **23% of marketers** are thinking about GEO

### Cold Outreach Playbook
1. Run geo-audit on 5 businesses in target niche
2. Generate PDF reports for each
3. Send cold email with PDF attached:
   > "I ran an AI visibility report on your website. You're scoring X/100. AI search engines aren't recommending you. Would you want to hop on a quick call?"

> "When a client sees that you're providing massive value for free, they want to hop on a call. This is the ultimate lead generation tool."

### Why It Works
- Value first — solving their problem before they pay
- Concrete data — they see exactly what's wrong
- Actionable plan — quick wins this week, medium-term this month
- Low barrier — don't have to change everything at once

## The 3-Step ChatGPT Traffic System

### Step 1: Get AI's Attention
- Create content so specific and authoritative AI can't ignore it
- Citation-worthy content with original research and data
- Optimize everything for AI parsing (clear structure, direct answers, scannable formatting)
- Submit site to Bing Webmaster tools (ChatGPT uses Bing's data)

### Step 2: Expand Your Authority
- Build mentions through strategic digital PR
- Get mentioned in context of solving specific problems
- Engage authentically in Reddit, Quora, industry forums
- Create comparison content (X vs Y vs Z) with real data

### Step 3: Own Your Space
- Use AI tools to create ultra-specific longtail content at scale
- Monitor mentions obsessively
- Track AI visibility with tools like Uber Suggest
- Test industry's common questions in ChatGPT monthly
- **Timeline: 3-6 months** to see real results

## Growth Metrics That Matter

### Old Metrics (Still Important But Not Differentiation)
- Traffic
- Rankings
- CTR

### New Metrics (The Real KPIs)
- **AI visibility score** — how often brand shows up in AI answers
- **Citation frequency** — how frequently content is sourced
- **Entity mention velocity** — how fast brand gains topical authority
- **Zeroclick value** — are you influencing decisions when people don't click?
- **Cross-platform presence** — does reputation travel across platforms?
