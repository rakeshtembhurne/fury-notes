# The Only Facebook Ads Tutorial You Need for 2025

## Key Insights

1. **Strong Offer Is Foundation** - Before touching Facebook ads, fix your offer. A compelling offer that solves one urgent problem makes the Meta algorithm work harder for you. Without it, CPMs skyrocket and CTR tanks— ads cost more because the system can't find your buyer efficiently.

2. **Use Meta Ad Library to Copy Winners** - Analyze competitor ads to identify what's working. The longer an ad runs, the higher probability it's generating positive ROI. Remix proven formats from multiple competitors rather than starting from scratch.

3. **Hook-Value-CTA Formula with Variations** - Create 3-5 hooks, 2-3 bodies, and multiple CTAs per ad. Test all combinations and remix weekly. Meta's AI finds winning combinations through AV testing, and variations prevent creative fatigue.

4. **Raw UGC Outperforms Polished Ads** - TikTok-style, low-fi content often outperforms highly-produced ads. UGC feels authentic and native, earning trust. Use a mix of UGC, carousels, mini-stories, and emotion-led graphics.

5. **Pixel + Conversion API for Proper Tracking** - iOS 14+ blocks browser pixel data. Server-side tracking via Conversion API is essential for Meta to learn who's actually buying. Clear conversion data teaches Meta's AI to optimize for your ideal customer.
