# Government Schemes — India (Maharashtra)

## Overview

Compilation of central and state government schemes for farmers and rural residents in Maharashtra, India. Primary portal: [mahadbt.maharashtra.gov.in](https://mahadbt.maharashtra.gov.in)

---

## Major State Schemes

### Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Krishi Swavalamban Yojana (BAKSY)
For SC/NT farmers. Maximum grant up to ₹4,00,000 for new wells, with 90% cost coverage for plastic-lined farm ponds.

Components and grant limits:
- New well: Up to ₹4,00,000
- Old well repair: ₹1,00,000
- Plastic-lined farm pond: 90% cost or ₹2,00,000 (whichever is less)
- Borewell, electricity connection, pump set, solar pump, HDPE/PVC pipes: ₹40,000–₹97,000
- Micro irrigation, farm machinery, backyard garden

Eligibility: 0.20 to 6 hectare landholders, SC/NT caste certificate required.
Apply: mahadbt.maharashtra.gov.in

### Namo Shetkari Mahasanman Nidhi (NSMNY)
Additional benefit for PM-KISAN beneficiaries — ₹6,000/year in three equal installments. No application needed; must be PM-KISAN beneficiary.

### Gopinath Munde Farmer Accident Safety Scheme
₹2,00,000 for farmer or family member (age 10–75) who dies or becomes permanently disabled due to accident. Submit documents to talathi office within 30 days.

## Central Government Schemes

### PM-KISAN (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi)
₹6,000/year in three installments to all eligible farmer families.

### PMFBY (Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana)
Crop insurance scheme.

### PDMC (Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana)
Micro irrigation support.

### MIDH (Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture)
Horticulture development support.

### MGNREGS
Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme — rural employment.

### Other Central Schemes
Agricultural mechanization, drones, solar pumps, greenhouse projects, fruit orchard expansion, seed production, food processing, agricultural awards, international study tours.

## Solar Drying / Post-Harvest Processing

### Solar Drying Machines
For drying grains, fruits, vegetables, medicinal plants, fish, turmeric, spices, grapes:
- Domestic units: ~₹40,000
- Larger units cost more
- Polycarbonate sheet greenhouse-type units available at 40% grant
- Post-harvest grant schemes include solar dryer/drying units

Benefits: Faster drying, dust-free, better quality than open sun drying.

---

## Key Notes

- Most schemes require online application via mahadbt.maharashtra.gov.in portal
- SC/NT farmers have access to the highest grant amounts (BAKSY up to ₹4 lakh)
- Solar drying equipment qualifies for 40% government grant under post-harvest schemes
- Documents required: caste certificate, land records, Aadhaar, bank account details
- For full details, contact your local taluka agriculture office
