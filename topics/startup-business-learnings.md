## Key Insights
- YC predicts 300+ unicorn vertical AI companies this decade
- Boring apps make real money: parking finders ($60K MRR), toll calculators ($45K MRR)
- 116 apps in 8 months making $100K+/month — most in boring categories nobody's heard of
- Solo founder: $0 to $30K MRR in 4 months via Reddit marketing
- Open-source SaaS viable: Postiz hit $43K MRR while fully open-source
- When selling a business, buyers care only if the company runs on its own
- The $97/month to $10K MRR to $1M exit in 12 months fantasy is unrealistic

## Viral Free Tool Marketing Loop
1. Build a free tool (grader, calculator, analyzer) that gives instant value
2. Results create sharing incentive (comparison, competition)
3. Tool markets your business forever -- SEO compounds
4. Upgrade path: free tool leads to paid solution for the problems it identified

## Solo Founder Playbook to $7K MRR
1. Set up alerts for every keyword in your niche ("one person everywhere" illusion)
2. Respond to everything on Twitter, Reddit, and relevant communities
3. Build in public daily -- share progress, revenue numbers, learnings
4. Post 1-3x a day with clear formatting
5. Reply to people in your community consistently
6. Repeat until you viral, then double down

## AI-First SaaS in 2026
1. Start with a big market (finance, healthcare, real estate), then zoom into a sub-niche
2. Map the niche's workflow end-to-end -- literally every step they do daily
3. Identify which steps AI can fully automate vs. augment
4. Build the automation layer, not just another dashboard
5. Price based on labor cost replaced, not software subscription
