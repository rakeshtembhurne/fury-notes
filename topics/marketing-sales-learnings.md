# Marketing & Sales Learnings

## Funnel Strategy
```
Ads (FB/IG) => Landing Page (Free Lead Magnet) => VSL Page (Rs 299 call) => Calendar (Book Call) => Product
```

**Lead Magnet:** Free Ebook → Email capture
**VSL:** Video Sales Letter → Padid Call for 299 (5% conversion)
**Calendar:** Book a call → Your product

## Client Persona (3 Levels)
- 3% BUYERS
- 17% Interested
- 80% Not Interested

## Niche-Problem-Solution Framework
- WHO = Who are you targeting
- Problem = What are you solving
- Solution = How you solve it

**Prompt:** Create a client persona of [target] who wants [outcome] through [medium]

## Credibility Triangle (3 As)
1. **Advantages** — What you have that customers lack (SOP, team, expertise, knowledge)
2. **Association** — Who you're connected to (celebrities, brands, companies)
3. **Authority** — What you've published (content: audio, video, PDF)

Increase each by 10% = 30% growth

## High-Ticket Sales Script
### CS-DS-GAP Framework
- **CS:** Current Situation (where they are)
- **DS:** Desired Situation (where they want to be)
- **GAP:** Bridge between CS and DS
- **Close:** Present the plan

> Why People Buy: GAP. Bigger the gap, more they pay.

### The 4 Variables
1. Reduce CS (make it sound worse)
2. Increase DS (make the dream bigger)
3. Break the Bridge (destroy their current plan)
4. Reduce Time (I can get you there in 2 months not 4)

### The Close
- "I have a plan, can I show you?"
- "Where should we go from here?"
- Present offer
- SHUT UP and wait

## Laws of Offer
1. **Story** — What story is behind this?
2. **Reward for Work** — Inner + Outer motivation
3. **Social Proof** — Who else is part of it (numbers matter)
4. **Celebrity Association** — Quality people/companies
5. **Naming** — They never buy BETTER, they buy DIFFERENT
6. **Bonus** — More value for same price
7. **Scarcity** — Time limit, limited units

> You need to be DIFFERENT not better.

## Content Machine (3 Hours/Month)
1. Identify WHO to target
2. Find 30 problems they face
3. Write 30 LinkedIn articles from problems
4. Write 30 Reel scripts (use Stop Video if no face)
5. Create 4 YouTube videos
6. Schedule via Publer
