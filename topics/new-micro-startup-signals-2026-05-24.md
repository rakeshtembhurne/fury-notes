### 📊 Strategy Signals

1. **Vanilla Stack Wins for AI Coding**
   - "Vanilla PHP + Vanilla JS + SQLite is so simple and basic it's hard for AI to fuck up"
   - The more complex your stack, the higher odds AI will make fatal bugs
   - Simple works here

2. **Agent Authority = New SEO**
   - "building authority for agents to discover and recommend your brand should be THE number one priority right now"
   - This is agentic SEO — optimize for AI agents, not just humans
   - Aligns with research_program's GEO focus

3. **$1M Grants for Agent-First Companies**
   - @chhddavid giving away $1M to agent-first companies
   - Opportunity: position Brandsome as agent-first

### 🛠 Tool Signals

1. **OpenAI Agent OS Features**
   - Mac control from phone (even when screen off)
   - App shots with live screen context
   - Goal Mode for long-running tasks (hours/days)
   - Advanced annotation for clearer instruction

2. **AI Moving from "Answer" to "Workflow"**
   - "AI is moving from 'answer my question' to 'run this workflow'"
   - Difference between using AI vs directing AI
   - Key insight for product design

### ❌ Lessons

1. **Weirdness is the Moat**
   - "Your weirdness is the moat. Your specific hard-to-pigeonhole point of view."
   - Don't sanitize your unique perspective

2. **Protect Revenue Before Making More**
   - "Sometimes protecting what you already have can be better than trying to make more"
   - Current revenue = foundation
