# Trigyaa 30-Day Instagram Content Calendar

> Based on the Trigyaa Playbook (projects/trigyaa-playbook.md)
> ICP: Urban working women 25-40 | Voice: dry, observational | Emoji: max 1/natural
> Channel priority: IG primary > Pinterest > WhatsApp > Blog

---

## Calendar Logic

**Content Mix (per week):**
- 3x Outfit of the Day (OOTD) posts — reach + engagement
- 2x Behind the Seam (BTS) posts — fabric/quality proof
- 1x Customer Love (UGC) — social proof
- 1x Styling Tips — value + wardrobe fatigue解决
- 1x Engagement/Reel (variety) — algorithm boost

**Posting Schedule (IST):**
- Reels: 9:00 AM IST (通勤前)
- Carousels: 12:00 PM IST (昼休み)
- Stories: 6:00 PM IST (下班后)

## Week 1: OOTD Focus — "A Week of Essentials"

### Day 1 (Monday)
**Content:** OOTD Carousel — "The Monday Shirt"
**Type:** 3-image carousel
**Caption hook:** "6:45 AM. Meeting at 9. This is the shirt I reach for."
**Pillar:** Outfit of the Day
**CTA:** "Link in bio for restock info"

### Day 2 (Tuesday)
**Content:** Behind the Seam Reel — fabric close-up
**Type:** 15-30 sec reel
**Hook:** "Zoom in. This is why it doesn't unravel at month 6."
**Pillar:** Behind the Seam
**Visual:** Macro shot of stitching, fabric texture, hem

### Day 3 (Wednesday)
**Content:** Styling Tips Carousel — "One piece, 3 occasions"
**Type:** 4-image carousel
**Hook:** "This kurta: brunch, office, date night. Same piece. Three hours of styling."
**Pillar:** Styling Tips
**CTA:** "Save this for your next shopping decision"

### Day 4 (Thursday)
**Content:** OOTD Reel — "Tuesday version"
**Type:** 20-40 sec reel
**Hook:** "What I actually wore to a 3-hour meeting + dinner."
**Pillar:** Outfit of the Day
**Visual:** Full outfit, ambient setting, real movement

### Day 5 (Friday)
**Content:** Customer Love Story
**Type:** Single image + story caption
**Hook:** "Priya's been wearing our linen shirt every Monday for 3 months. Here's the wash count."
**Pillar:** Customer Love
**CTA:** "Share your Trigyaa story — tag us"

### Day 6 (Saturday)
**Content:** Engagement Post — Poll/Question
**Type:** Story poll or carousel
**Hook:** "What's your biggest laundry frustration? Vote below."
**Pillar:** Community engagement
**Goal:** Story poll increase reach

### Day 7 (Sunday)
**Content:** Behind the Seam — "Why this fabric"
**Type:** Carousel (2-3 slides)
**Hook:** "Most tops shrink. Because the yarn is pre-shrunk for 65°C wash. Ours is for 40°C. That's why."
**Pillar:** Behind the Seam
**Goal:** Education + quality signal

## Key Rules for All Posts

1. **Voice:** Dry, observational. Never "slay queen" or "fire 🔥"
2. **Emoji:** Max 1 per post. Natural only (🧵, ✂️, 🌿). No 🔥💅✨
3. **Caption length:** 50-150 words. Lead with the hook, not the product.
4. **Images:** Real, not stock. Ambient light. Indian women in real settings.
5. **Hashtags:** Max 5. Specific, not generic. (#Trigyaa, #MinimalistWardrobe, #IndianWomenFashion)
6. **CTA:** Always include. "Link in bio", "DM for sizing", "Share your story"
