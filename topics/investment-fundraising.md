# Investment & Fundraising

## Overview

Fundraising is a necessary evil for those wanting to build and scale large companies. Many household brands wouldn't exist without outside help — but bootstrapped businesses can also be hugely successful (e.g., MailChimp sold for $12B to Intuit).

---

## Key Insight

> "Ask any maker who's been through it — fundraising is hard. In some cases, outright brutal."

## Key Takeaways

1. **Bootstrapping is a valid path** — MailChimp ($12B exit) proves external funding isn't the only way
2. **Fundraising is brutal** — emotionally and logistically demanding
3. **Tools exist to help** — CRMs, template decks, warm intro networks
4. **Due diligence matters** — have your docs ready before approaching investors
5. **VC questions are predictable** — 120+ common questions exist, prepare for them
