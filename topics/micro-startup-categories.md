## AI Agents for Micro-Startups — Is This Real?

### The Market Reality

**What we think we're building:** AI agents that autonomously run micro-startups

**What the data shows:**
- No indie hackers are selling "AI business agents"
- Successful AI products are VERTICAL (Chatbase, Gojiberry)
- The successful pattern: **Use AI to do ONE thing well**

### What People Are Actually Buying

| AI Product | Revenue | What It Does |
|-----------|---------|--------------|
| Chatbase | $10M ARR | AI chatbots for websites |
| Gojiberry.ai | 2K+ customers | AI something (unspecified) |
| T3 Chat | Unknown | AI chat app |

**Key insight:** Vertical AI tools > Horizontal AI agents for indie hackers

### Pricing Evidence

- **Claude Code:** $200/mo (too expensive for many)
- **Codex:** Integrated into Copilot (~$10/mo or included)
- **Cursor:** $20/mo pro tier
- **AI business agents:** No market data found

**Verdict:** "AI agents for micro-startups" is NOT a verified market. Indie hackers are using AI tools to BUILD products, not selling AI to run businesses.
