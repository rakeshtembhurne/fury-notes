# Strategic Pivot — What Changed

## What We Originally Said

> "Build AI systems that run micro-startups fully autonomously"

## What We Learned

From analyzing 5,964 tweets + gap analysis:

1. **No market exists for "AI business agents"**
   - No indie hackers are selling this
   - The successful AI products are vertical (Chatbase, Gojiberry)
   - People buy AI that does ONE thing well

2. **Successful indie hackers do the OPPOSITE**
   - @levelsio: Ships Hoodmaps (real maps)
   - @marclou: 33 startups solving small problems
   - @robj3d3: SuperX (Twitter growth tool)
   - They use AI to BUILD, not to run businesses

3. **AI is a tool, not a product**
   - The product must solve a real problem
   - AI makes you build faster, not replaces the need for a product

## What We Pivoted To

**Original:** AI that runs micro-startups
**New:** Micro-startups that use AI as competitive advantage

## What This Means

1. We still USE AI (our Hermes system, vibe coding)
2. But our PRODUCTS are micro-startups, not AI tools
3. We validate before building (tweet first)
4. We use TrustMRR as exit strategy

## Why This Is Better

1. **Validated market** — Micro-startups buying/selling is real
2. **Clear path to revenue** — Build → MRR → Sell
3. **Leverages our strengths** — Research system, AI agent
4. **Reduces risk** — 33 startups = diversified portfolio

## What We Need To Do

1. **Find ONE category** to focus on first
2. **Validate with a tweet** before building
3. **Build in a weekend** with Codex
4. **Get first customer in 2 weeks**
