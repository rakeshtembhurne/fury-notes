# Complete AI YouTube Video Creation Workflow - 2026

## Key Insights

1. **Find Validated Ideas First** - Don't create from scratch. Use YouTube in incognito mode to find growing channels (10-100 subscribers), extract their top video transcripts, and reverse-engineer what's working.

2. **Two Workflow Paths** - (A) Magic Light: Fast, automated, ~5-15 min per video, good for scaling. (B) Manual: Cinematic quality, ~2-4 hours per video, for high-end courses/tutorials. Choose based on your goals.

3. **Transcripts Are Gold** - Replace `/watch?v=` with `/transcript?v=` in any YouTube URL to get the full transcript. Analyze these with ChatGPT to find viral hooks, structure, and psychological triggers.

4. **Consistency Beats Quality Initially** - Start producing, then improve over time. Don't over-engineer your first version. The key is publishing consistently and iterating based on data.

5. **Prompt Writing Mastery** - For image-to-video workflows, include: character, description, environment, lighting, camera angle, and mood. Use ChatGPT to generate consistent character prompts across scenes.
