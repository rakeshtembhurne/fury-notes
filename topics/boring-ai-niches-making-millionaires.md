# The "Boring" AI Niches Making Millionaires in 2026

## Key Stats
- Software market: $650 billion
- US labor market: $10 trillion
- Currently automated: 0.2% of labor market
- AI is the first technology that can automate labor

## The Opportunity
- Most of the AI application layer is still unwritten
- AI needs to be SPECIALIZED for specific industries
- Just like steam engines → factory conveyor belts
- Just like software → CRM, apps
- AI needs to be specialized → industry-specific tools

## Why "Boring" Niches Win
- Less competition
- Clear pain point
- Higher willingness to pay
- More defensible

## Key Examples
- YC startups raising millions in previously "too small" markets
- AI SEO software → $1M ARR in < 2 years
- AI agencies scaling fast in niche verticals

## Key Insight
> "AI is like magic, but it's also messy. Businesses can't deal with black boxes, hallucinations, and error-prone workflows."

## Strategy for Brandsome
- Don't try to be everything to everyone
- Pick a specific vertical (e.g., fashion brands, local businesses)
- Build for that niche specifically
- Expand after finding product-market fit

## Best Paths
1. AI Agency (even if not a coder)
2. Niche AI SaaS
3. Vertical-specific AI tools
